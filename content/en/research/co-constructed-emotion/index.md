---
title: Co-Construction of Emotion through Social Interaction
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - emotion-model
---

We computationally model the process by which multiple agents **co-construct** emotion categories through social interaction, using collective predictive coding and Metropolis–Hastings naming games. Built on multimodal deep generative models, we aim to constructively clarify how emotion symbols emerge and are shared among embodied agents.
