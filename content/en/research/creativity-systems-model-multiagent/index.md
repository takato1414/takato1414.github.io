---
title: Multi-Agent Simulation of the Systems Model of Creativity
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - creativity-model
---

We implement Csikszentmihalyi's *systems model of creativity* (the interaction of individual, field, and domain) as an *in silico* simulation by multiple generative-AI-driven agents, and constructively clarify how creativity emerges on a social system. We further address the co-evolution of generative strategies and evaluative preferences in AI music (cultural drift), aiming at a multifaceted understanding of creative dynamics in agent populations.
