---
title: Development and Application of Soft Tactile Sensors
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - hri
---

We develop **soft tactile sensors** that enable rich bodily contact between robots and humans/environments. From multi-axis force sensors that combine magnetorheological elastomers with inductors, to flow-channel-based soft sensors using liquid metal, to optical sensors that measure surface deformation, we explore novel combinations of deformable materials and transduction principles, applying them to robots.
