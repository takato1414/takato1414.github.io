---
title: Toddler Temperament Estimation from Robot Interaction Data
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - hri
---

Through **explainable machine learning (XAI)**, we analyze behavioral data from child-robot interactions to estimate toddlers' temperament and personality. By clarifying in which interaction styles or scenarios individual differences emerge most strongly, we aim to support developmental and educational applications of robots tailored to each child.
