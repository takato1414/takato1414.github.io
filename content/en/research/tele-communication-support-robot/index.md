---
title: Tele-Communication Support Robots
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - hri
---

We study how the embodiment and dialogue design of robots can support communication between geographically separated people. From **remote childcare robots** that scaffold infant language development to **"live-commentary play"** techniques that ease initial relationship-building among unfamiliar partners, we constructively clarify the role and effect of robots that stand between people.
