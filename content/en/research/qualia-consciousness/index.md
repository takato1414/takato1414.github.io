Can robots have emotional qualia? Addressing this question, we work on elucidating the correspondence between qualia structures and information structures. As part of the Transformative Research Areas (A) project on "Qualia Structurology," we are building neural circuit models of subjective experience and frameworks based on probabilistic generative models. Joint research with IRCN at The University of Tokyo is also underway.
