---
title: Shared Autonomy for Brain-Machine-Interface Teleoperation of Robots
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - hri
---

Intent signals from brain-machine interfaces (BMIs) are inherently low-bandwidth and noisy, making fine-grained robot control difficult. We build **shared autonomy** frameworks in which the robot adaptively complements user actions based on **goal estimation**, aiming to enhance the practical usability of BMI-driven robot teleoperation and navigation.
