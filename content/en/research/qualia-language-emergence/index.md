---
title: Toward a Constructive Understanding of Emotion Structure and Language Emergence
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - qualia-consciousness
---

We constructively investigate the **bidirectional influence** between the structure of subjective experience — emotion and qualia — and the emergence of language among populations of agents. Using the framework of symbol-emergence systems, we explore through computational models how internal emotion/qualia structures shape language, and how shared language in turn reshapes individual experiential structures.
