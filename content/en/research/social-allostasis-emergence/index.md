---
title: Emergence and Sharing of Social Reality via Allostasis through Social Interaction
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - social-reality-model
---

Based on **allostasis** (predictive regulation for maintaining homeostasis) mediated by social communication, we computationally model how the "social reality" of emotion emerges and is shared among agents. Through a social allostasis model with dynamic interpretants, we aim to constructively clarify how affective meaning is formed and shared through the interplay between social interaction and internal-state regulation.
