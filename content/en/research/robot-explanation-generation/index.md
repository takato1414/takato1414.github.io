---
title: Explanation Generation for Autonomous Robot Decision-Making
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - hri
---

We build frameworks that let autonomous robots **explain** their decision-making processes and the underlying reasoning to people in an understandable way. Through distributed representations of world models, we visualize the robot's internal states and its estimates of others' world models, supporting reliable human-robot collaboration.
