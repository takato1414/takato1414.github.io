---
title: Affective Communication via Active Inference
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - emotion-model
---

Based on **active inference** and the free-energy principle, we computationally model multimodal affective communication between robots and humans. Integrating energy-minimization-driven active perception by the robot with emotion estimation and expression through mental simulation, we build frameworks in which the robot reads and responds to others' emotions.
