---
title: Co-Creative Music Generation through Interactive Preference Estimation
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - creativity-model
---

We study **co-creative music generation** that estimates users' affective preferences on the fly through dialogue and reflects them in diverse creative suggestions. Using In-Context Learning and In-Context Preference Learning, we take in value criteria without additional training and aim to support humans and AI in collaboratively creating new musical expressions.
