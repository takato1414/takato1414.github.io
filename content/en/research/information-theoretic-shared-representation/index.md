---
title: Information-Theoretic Analysis of Shared Representations Between Agents
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - qualia-consciousness
---

We analyze the structure of **shared representations** that multiple agents acquire through interaction, using information-theoretic approaches including Integrated Information Theory (IIT). We investigate the conditions under which representations shift from being "inside individual heads" to being "shared across multiple heads," and characterize the resulting information structures through computational models and simulations.
