---
title: Multi-Robot Coordination with Large Language Models
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - action-planning
---

We integrate the commonsense reasoning of large language models (LLMs) with **structured optimization** such as linear programming and dependency graphs, enabling cooperative task planning by multiple robots. The LLM decomposes natural-language tasks, while inter-robot dependencies and constraints are solved through optimization to produce feasible cooperative behaviors even under real-world constraints.
