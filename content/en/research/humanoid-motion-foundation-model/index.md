---
title: Foundation Models for Humanoid Motion Learning
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - motor-skill-learning
---

We apply **foundation models** such as GPT to humanoid motor control, building unified policies that handle diverse motion tasks including locomotion and whole-body actions. By leveraging the general-purpose reasoning of pretrained models and refining them with action data, we aim at data-efficient, multi-task humanoid control.
