---
title: Multimodal Instruction Foundation Models with Mask Images
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - motor-skill-learning
---

We extend vision-language-action (VLA) foundation models with **attention-guided mask images**, making the correspondence between natural-language and visual instructions explicit so that robots can accurately capture instruction targets and act on them. Through Alpha-channel attention control, we aim at action selection consistent with both language and vision even in cluttered environments.
