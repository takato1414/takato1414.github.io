---
title: Mind Synchrony and Emergence of Social Reality through Asymmetric Interaction
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - social-reality-model
---

Using **asymmetric social interactions** — exemplified by parent-infant exchanges — as the subject, we computationally model how mind (internal-representation) synchrony is established through collective predictive coding, and how social reality emerges from it. Grounded in constructive theories of emotion, we explore mechanisms of dynamic representational synchrony through homeostatic co-regulation.
