---
title: Modeling Social Norm Formation and Creativity via Active Inference
date: 2024-01-01
tags:
  - social-reality-model
---

Using **active inference** as a unifying framework, we computationally model how social norms form and are maintained through agent interactions, and how the same predictive processing also produces creative behavior — productive deviation from norms. We aim to capture the dialectical relationship between conformity and creativity in a single predictive framework.
