I will give a talk titled **"Connecting Bodies, Connecting Predictions: Bridging Physical AI and Developmental Models through Cognitive Developmental Robotics"** in Symposium 1 of the **15th Annual Meeting of the Japan Society for Developmental Neuroscience**, held on November 7–8, 2026 at the Ibuka Memorial Hall, Waseda University.

<!--more-->

The symposium explores the intersection of cognitive developmental robotics and developmental neuroscience. I will be joined on the panel by Kento Kawaharazuka, Emiko Uchiyama, and Shingo Murata.

For details, see the [official conference page](https://jsdn.jp/meeting-information/15th-2026).
