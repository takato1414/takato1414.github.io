I will be a panelist in the organized session **"Semiotics of Life and Purpose"** (chair: Hitoshi Sako, Tokyo Transportation Junior College) at the **46th Annual Conference of the Japanese Association for Semiotic Studies — "Frontiers of Peircean Semiotics"**, held on July 11–12, 2026 at the Kudan Campus of Nishogakusha University, Tokyo.

<!--more-->

The session features topic contributions by Norihiko Kamaya (Hokkaido University) and Takato Horii, exploring "life" and "purpose" from a semiotic perspective across biosemiotics, life studies, and cognitive science / robotics.

For details, see the [official conference page](https://www.jassweb.jp/46taikai).
