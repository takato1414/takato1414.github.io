Our lab will give the following four presentations at the Robotics and Mechatronics Conference 2026 ([ROBOMECH 2026](https://robomech.org/2026/), June 28 – July 1, 2026, Fukuoka International Congress Center).

<!--more-->

**[2A1-C05] Acquisition of Conversational Formation Participation Behavior for Humanoids via VLA**
*Takashi Kato, Tatsuya Aoki, Yuichiro Yoshikawa, Takato Horii (Osaka Univ.)*

**[2A1-B10] Effects of Natural Walking Motion and Footstep Sounds of Humanoids on Gait Synchronization and Interpersonal Impressions**
*Motoki Hagiwara, Kazuki Miyazawa, Takato Horii, Hiroshi Ishiguro (Osaka Univ.)*

**[2A2-T07] MAITET: Development of an Affordable Modular Whole-Body Multi-Joint Exoskeleton for Humanoids**
*Yuta Kuroda, Kazuki Miyazawa, Takato Horii (Osaka Univ.)*

**[2A2-U03] Acquisition of Quiet Walking Motion for Humanoids**
*Ukyo Abe, Kazuki Miyazawa, Takato Horii (Osaka Univ.)*
