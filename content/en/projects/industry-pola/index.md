In the cosmetics and skin-care domain, this joint research scientifically clarifies the subjective and affective experiences such as "pleasantness" and "feel of the skin," combining psychophysical measurements with computational models.

Building on our work in cognitive developmental robotics and tactile interaction, we model the correspondence between tactile stimuli on the skin and perceptual experiences using generative models and representation learning. We aim to quantify affective values and support product design that reflects individual differences.
